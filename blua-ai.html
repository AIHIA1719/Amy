9:42
This is an important question. In general, ibuprofen can interfere with some blood pressure medications and may cause your blood pressure to rise.
  • It can reduce the effectiveness of ACE inhibitors and diuretics
  • It may cause fluid retention
  • Paracetamol is usually a safer alternative for pain relief

Signs of anxiety I should know about
Clinically reviewed
3 min read
Overview
Anxiety is very common and shows up differently for different people. Knowing what to look out for is the first step to getting the right support.
Common signs
🧠
Mental symptoms
Excessive worry, racing thoughts, difficulty concentrating, feeling on edge or irritable
💓
Physical symptoms
Increased heart rate, sweating, trembling, shortness of breath, fatigue, headaches
😴
Sleep disturbance
Difficulty falling asleep, waking during the night, or restless and unrefreshing sleep
When to seek help
If symptoms have lasted more than two weeks and are affecting your daily life, it's worth speaking to a GP. You don't have to manage this alone.

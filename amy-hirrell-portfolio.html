About me

Design that's felt,
not just seen

I'm Amy — a Lead Product Designer with 20+ years of experience spanning TV, responsive web, app, motion, branding, illustration, and everything in between. I bring rigour to research, clarity to strategy, and real craft to every screen I ship.

Currently Lead UI Designer at Sky (10+ years), where I've helped shape products used by millions — including Sky Q. Previous work includes British Gas, Hive, DDB Bahrain, and contract work for the London Transport Museum, Disney, Boots, and Sainsbury's Energy.

I won a British Gas IS Award for creating their first responsive Digital Style Guide and Pattern Library. I design with empathy and deliver with impact.

How I work

A process built for
real-world complexity

01

Discover

I start with the problem, not the solution. User interviews, competitor analysis, data deep-dives, and stakeholder alignment to build a shared picture of what we're actually solving.

02

Define

Synthesis into clear problem statements, opportunity areas, and design principles. Prioritisation frameworks to focus on the highest-impact work first.

03

Design

Rapid conceptual sketches through to high-fidelity prototypes. Iteration with users at every stage. Accessibility, edge cases, and engineering constraints baked in from day one.

04

Deliver

Close collaboration with engineering through to launch. Post-launch measurement, iteration cycles, and building on what we learn to make each release better than the last.
